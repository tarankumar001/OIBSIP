Muhammad Ali

The Greatest Boxer of All Time

About Him

Muhammad Ali (born Cassius Clay) was an American professional boxer, activist, and cultural icon. Known for his speed, courage, and personality, he is widely regarded as the greatest boxer in history.

Key Achievements

  • 3-time World Heavyweight Champion
  • 1960 Olympic Gold Medalist
  • Won 56 out of 61 professional fights
  • Famous for “Float like a butterfly, sting like a bee”
  • Strong voice for civil rights and peace

Famous Quote

"I hated every minute of training, but I said, ‘Don’t quit. Suffer now and live the rest of your life as a champion.’"
